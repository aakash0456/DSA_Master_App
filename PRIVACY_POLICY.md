# Privacy Policy — DSA Master

_Last updated: July 2026_

DSA Master is a fully offline study app.

- **We do not collect, store, transmit, sell or share any personal data.**
- The app has no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no accounts and no network features.
- Everything you create (custom flashcards, notes, progress, settings) is stored only in the app's private storage on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
- The optional daily reminder is scheduled locally on your device and requires the notification permission, which you can revoke at any time in system settings.
- The app does not request internet, location, contacts, camera or storage permissions.

If Android's automatic backup is enabled on your device, your local app data may be included in your own Google account device backup, controlled entirely by your device settings — the app developer has no access to it.
